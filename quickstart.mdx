## Setup

Follow these steps to get started with Pauv.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [pauv.com](https://pauv.com) to get access.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install dependencies">
    Install the required dependencies for your project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your environment">
    Set up your environment variables and configuration files.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start building">
    You're ready to start building with Pauv!
  </Step>
</Steps>
