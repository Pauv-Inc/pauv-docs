## What is a Talent?

A **Talent** is any public figure — actor, musician, athlete, creator, or celebrity — who lists themselves on Pauv. When you list as a Talent, users can acquire your PVs, and you earn ongoing royalties from the market activity around your profile.

Pauv will never create PVs or list any individual without that person's consent.

## How Talent Earns

Talent earns two streams of income, both paid as **USD deposits to your linked bank account monthly**:

### 1. Transaction Royalty — 0.25% of All Trades

Every time a user buys or sells your PVs, you receive **0.25% of the transaction value**. This applies to both buy and sell orders.

### 2. Market Cap Royalty — 1% Annual

You receive **1% annually** of the total USDP held in your PV market capitalization, calculated based on an **Average Daily Balance (ADB)** and paid monthly.

| Income Stream | Rate | Paid |
|---------------|------|------|
| Transaction royalty | 0.25% per trade | Monthly (USD) |
| Market cap royalty | 1% annual (ADB) | Monthly (USD) |

<Note>
  Both royalty streams are paid in **USD fiat** to your bank account — not in USDP or PVs. You are free to use your earnings however you wish, including buying your own PVs on the platform if you choose.
</Note>

## Getting Listed

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your Talent profile">
    Sign up at [pauv.com](https://pauv.com) and apply to list as Talent. Provide your profile image, social media handles, and bio information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Standard KYC">
    Before your profile goes live, you must pass identity verification (Simple KYC) through Soap Payments. This includes name, date of birth, address verification, and AML screening.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your PVs go live">
    Once approved, your PVs are available for users to buy and sell. Your PV starts at $0.01 and follows the same transparent linear pricing curve as all Talents.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Step-Up KYC to withdraw">
    To withdraw your earnings, you must complete enhanced verification: uploading a government-issued photo ID and completing a selfie liveness check. No funds leave the ecosystem without this step.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## No Fiduciary Duty

Listing on Pauv does **not** create a fiduciary relationship between you and PV holders. You do not owe PV holders any financial returns, performance obligations, or special treatment.

## Minor Talent

Individuals under 18 may be listed as Talent **only** with verifiable parental or guardian consent:

- A parent or legal guardian must create and manage the account
- The guardian must pass KYC verification and sign a legal agreement
- The guardian accepts full legal responsibility for the Minor's participation
- The parent/guardian can review the Minor's information, request deletion, or revoke consent at any time

## Grant of Rights

By listing as Talent, you grant Pauv a non-exclusive, worldwide, royalty-free license to use the name, image, likeness, and username you provide for the purpose of identifying your PVs and marketing the platform.
