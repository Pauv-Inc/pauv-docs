## What is USDP?

USDP is Pauv's internal, dollar-denominated ledger credit. It is the only currency used to buy and sell PVs on the platform.

| Property | Detail |
|----------|--------|
| **Exchange rate** | 1 USDP = $1 USD (always) |
| **Transferable?** | No — USDP cannot be sent to other users, platforms, or wallets |
| **Cryptocurrency?** | No — USDP is an internal ledger entry, not a blockchain token |
| **External value?** | None — USDP has no value outside of pauv.com |

Think of USDP like casino chips: you exchange real dollars for them to use within the platform, and you can exchange them back when you're done.

## Depositing Funds

When you deposit USD into Pauv:

1. Your funds are processed through **Finix** (ACH/card processor) and **Soap Payments** (security gateway)
2. Your USD is deposited into a **regulated FBO (For Benefit Of) custodial account** at Lead Bank via BVNK
3. Your Pauv wallet is credited with the equivalent amount in USDP

<Warning>
  Pauv **never** commingles user funds with corporate operating funds. Your dollars are held separately in regulated custodial accounts at all times.
</Warning>

## Using USDP

All trading on Pauv happens in USDP:

- **Buy PVs** — Spend USDP to acquire a Talent's PVs at the current algorithmic price
- **Sell PVs** — Sell PVs back to receive USDP at the current price
- **Instant settlement** — Because USDP is an internal ledger entry, trades execute instantly without relying on external payment rails

## Withdrawing Funds

You can withdraw your USDP balance back to USD at any time:

1. Initiate a withdrawal request through the platform
2. Pauv performs a **ledger-to-blockchain reconciliation** to verify your transaction history and balance
3. Funds are remitted to your **linked bank account** within **1–2 business days**

<Note>
  Withdrawals go back to the same verified bank account you used to deposit. You cannot withdraw to a different account without updating your verification.
</Note>

## Custody & Security

- **FBO Accounts** — All user funds are held in For Benefit Of custodial accounts at Lead Bank, a regulated U.S. depository institution
- **No commingling** — User funds are never mixed with Pauv's operational accounts
- **Encryption** — All financial records are protected with AES-256 encryption in transit and at rest
- **Audit trail** — Every deposit, trade, and withdrawal is recorded on Pauv's private blockchain for tamper-proof auditability

## Interest on Custodial Funds

Funds held in FBO custodial accounts may earn interest. Per the [Terms of Service](/legal/terms-of-service), any interest or earnings on pooled custodial balances ("float") are the sole property of Pauv Inc. Users have no claim to these earnings.
