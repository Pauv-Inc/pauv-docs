## Create Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Visit [pauv.com](https://pauv.com) and create your account with your name, email, and a secure password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your identity (KYC)">
    Before you can deposit funds or trade, you must complete identity verification through our secure partner, Soap Payments. 
    
    **Standard verification includes:**
    - Name, date of birth, and address verification against authoritative databases
    - SMS verification of your mobile device
    - Real-time AML screening against global watchlists

    This process is quick and typically completes in under a minute.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link your bank account">
    Connect your U.S. bank account to enable deposits and withdrawals. Only U.S.-based accounts are supported.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deposit USD">
    Load funds via ACH or card. Your USD is held securely in a regulated FBO custodial account, and your Pauv balance is credited with USDP at a 1:1 ratio.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start trading">
    Browse Talent profiles, research public sentiment, and acquire PVs for the people you believe in. All trades execute instantly against the transparent linear pricing curve.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Eligibility

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Age** | Must be 18 or older to trade |
| **Residency** | United States only |
| **Verification** | Must pass KYC identity verification |

<Note>
  Individuals under 18 may be listed as **Talent** on the platform with verifiable parental consent, but they cannot trade PVs or hold USDP.
</Note>

## Step-Up Verification

In certain cases, you may be asked to complete enhanced verification (Step-Up KYC). This involves uploading a government-issued photo ID and completing a selfie liveness check.

**Step-Up KYC is triggered when:**
- A Talent wants to withdraw earnings to their bank account
- Our risk engine detects device anomalies or elevated risk signals
- Deposit activity triggers enhanced review thresholds

## Account Security

You are responsible for keeping your login credentials secure. If you suspect unauthorized access to your account:

1. Email **support@pauv.com** immediately with "URGENT: ACCOUNT SECURITY" in the subject line
2. Your account will be temporarily frozen to prevent unauthorized activity
3. Our team will verify your identity and work with you to secure your account

## Tips for New Users

- **Start small** — Familiarize yourself with the platform and pricing model before committing significant funds.
- **Understand the model** — PV prices move based on buy/sell activity. Read the [How It Works](/how-it-works) page to understand the linear pricing curve.
- **PVs are not investments** — Treat PVs as digital collectibles for entertainment and engagement. Do not trade with funds you cannot afford to lose.
- **Withdrawals take 1–2 business days** — This is standard processing time for ledger-to-blockchain reconciliation.
