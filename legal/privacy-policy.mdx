*Last Updated: March 08, 2026*

Pauv Inc. ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you visit pauv.com (the "Platform") or use our services. By using the Platform, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect three categories of information:

### A. Voluntary Data (Information You Provide)

- **Account Registration:** Name, email address, username, and password
- **Talent Profile:** If you list yourself as Talent, we collect the profile image, social media handles, and bio information you choose to display publicly
- **Verification Data (KYC/KYB):** Government ID numbers, physical addresses, and dates of birth — facilitated securely via Soap Payments
- **Support:** Information sent to us when you contact customer support

### B. Automatic Data (Information Collected via Technology)

- **Device & Usage:** IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, and timestamps
- **Activity:** Pages viewed, time spent on the Platform, and interface interactions
- **Cookies:** We use cookies and local storage to maintain your login session and ensure platform security

### C. Transaction Data

- **Financial History:** Records of fiat deposits, USDP balances, PV trades, royalty disbursements, and withdrawals
- **Ledger Records:** Transaction metadata is recorded immutably on our internal hybrid database and private ledger

## 2. Third-Party Service Providers

We share necessary data with trusted partners to provide services and comply with financial regulations:

- **Payment & Identity Processing (Soap Payments & Finix):** Pauv does not store your full credit card number or bank account routing details. Financial routing and KYC/KYB verification are managed by Soap Payments and Finix.
- **Banking Rails (BVNK & Lead Bank):** Your fiat funds are held in FBO accounts. Transaction metadata is shared securely via API.
- **Partner Policies:** Your financial and identity data will be processed by these partners in accordance with their respective Privacy Policies and federal banking standards.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

- **Service Delivery:** To create your account, process USDP transactions, execute PV trades, and disburse Talent royalties
- **Security & AML Compliance:** To detect wash trading, market manipulation, account takeovers, and fulfill Bank Secrecy Act obligations (including OFAC screening, SARs, and CTRs)
- **Communication:** To send transaction confirmations, security alerts, and administrative updates

## 4. Your Rights (Texas TDPSA Compliance)

Under the Texas Data Privacy and Security Act (TDPSA), you have the following rights:

| Right | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Access** | Request a copy of the personal data we maintain about you |
| **Correction** | Request that we correct inaccuracies in your personal data |
| **Deletion** | Request that we delete personal data you have provided (financial and KYC records must be retained up to 7 years per AML/BSA law) |
| **Portability** | Request a copy of your data in a digital, machine-readable format |
| **Appeal** | Appeal our decision if we deny your request |

**To exercise your rights:** Email **support@pauv.com**. We will authenticate your identity and respond within 45 days.

## 5. Data Security & Retention

- **Security:** We implement industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS) and bank-grade security protocols for data in transit and at rest.
- **Retention:** Personal information is retained only as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations. Financial and identity records are stored securely for up to 7 years.

## 6. Children's Privacy & Minors

- **Traders:** Trading features are strictly for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18 for trading accounts.
- **Minor Talent (COPPA Notice):** Individuals under 18 may be listed as Talent only with verifiable parental consent.
  - We collect the Minor's name, image, and biographical details solely to create their public profile.
  - We require the parent/guardian to pass KYC verification and sign a legal agreement.
  - The parent/guardian may review the Minor's information, request deletion, and revoke consent at any time by contacting **support@pauv.com**.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the Platform after changes constitutes acceptance.
