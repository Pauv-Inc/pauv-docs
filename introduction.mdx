## What is Pauv?

Pauv is the world's first marketplace for human potential. We provide a digital platform that allows users to open a position on the public sentiment of rising talent — actors, musicians, athletes, creators, and celebrities — by acquiring digital collectibles known as **Personal Value units (PVs)**.

Think of it like trading cards, but for real people's cultural trajectory. When you believe in someone's potential, you can back them by acquiring their PVs. As public interest grows, so does the value of those PVs.

## Core Concepts

- **PVs (Personal Value units)** — Non-transferable digital collectibles tied to a Talent's perceived cultural or professional trajectory. PVs are **not** equity, stock, or cryptocurrency.
- **USDP** — An internal, dollar-denominated ledger credit (1 USDP = $1 USD) used to buy and sell PVs on the platform. USDP has no value outside of Pauv.
- **Talent** — The public figures (athletes, musicians, actors, creators) who list themselves on Pauv and earn ongoing royalties from their PV market activity.

## Key Features

- **Transparent Pricing** — All PV prices are determined by a public, continuous linear growth formula. No hidden market makers.
- **Closed-Loop Ecosystem** — No peer-to-peer transfers. No external wallets. All trades happen directly against Pauv's pricing curve.
- **Constant Liquidity** — The algorithmic pricing model ensures you can always buy or sell PVs at the current market price.
- **Bank-Grade Security** — User funds are held in regulated FBO custodial accounts, never commingled with Pauv's operating funds.
- **Regulatory Compliance** — Pauv is a registered Money Services Business (MSB) with FinCEN, with full KYC/AML compliance.
